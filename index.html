About me

I have a strong interest in (sub)mesoscale turbulence in the ocean and how they interact with the large-scale ocean circulation and the biosphere particularly in the Southern Ocean. I approach the problems by analyzing data outputs from general circulation models, idealized numerical simulations and satelite observations.
